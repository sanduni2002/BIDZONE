Auction Policy



1.Registration:

• Users must register on our website before participating in auctions.

• Registration requires providing accurate personal information, including name, address, and contact details.

• Users must be at least 18 years old to register and participate in auctions.


2.Item Listings:

• Sellers are responsible for providing accurate and detailed information about the items they list for auction.

• Items should be accurately described, including any defects, condition, or special characteristics.

• Sellers should upload clear and high-quality images of the items to showcase their features.


3.Bidding:

• Bidders must carefully review item descriptions, images, and any additional terms and conditions set by the seller before placing a bid.

• Bidders can place bids above the current highest bid or use automatic bidding features, if available.

• Bids are binding and cannot be retracted or modified once placed.

• Bidders should refrain from engaging in any fraudulent or manipulative bidding practices.


4.Winning and Payment:

• The highest bidder at the end of the auction will be declared the winner.

• Winners are obligated to complete the purchase and make payment for the item won, including any additional fees, such as shipping or taxes.

• Payment methods accepted will be clearly specified on the website.

• Payment must be made within a specified timeframe to secure the item.

5.Disputes and Resolution:

• In case of disputes between bidders or between bidders and sellers, users should contact our customer support for assistance.

• We encourage users to resolve disputes amicably and in good faith. However, if a resolution cannot be reached, our team will intervene and make a fair decision.


6.Prohibited Activities:

• Users are strictly prohibited from engaging in any illegal, fraudulent, or unethical activities on our platform.

• Any attempts to manipulate bidding, defraud other users, or engage in any form of misconduct may result in account suspension or legal action.


7.Privacy and Data Protection:

8.Amendments to the Policy: *

• Users will be notified of any changes through our website or via email.
